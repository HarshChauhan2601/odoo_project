Monthly Strength Training Plan

June 2024
Sun Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri Sat

Whole Day Plan

Morning
Wake Up (6:00 AM)

Hydrate: Drink a glass of water.
Light Snack (Optional): If you prefer to eat before working out,
have a small snack like a banana or a handful of nuts.
Morning Workout (6:30 AM)

Warm-Up (5 minutes): Dynamic stretches and light cardio (jumping
jacks, high knees). Strength Training (30 minutes): Full-body routine focusing on
major muscle groups.
Compound Exercises:
Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
Bent-over rows (using dumbbells or resistance bands): 3 sets of
10 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
Planks: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds
Cool Down (5 minutes): Light stretching.
Post-Workout Nutrition (7:15 AM)

High-Protein Breakfast:
Example: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast, or a
protein smoothie with Greek yogurt, spinach, and berries.
Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM)
Protein and Carbs:
Example: Greek yogurt with a small handful of nuts and fruit, or

a protein bar.
Lunch (12:30 PM)
Balanced Meal:
Lean Protein: Grilled chicken, fish, or tofu.
Complex Carbs: Brown rice, quinoa, or sweet potatoes.
Vegetables: A large portion of mixed vegetables.
Healthy Fats: Avocado or olive oil dressing on a salad.
Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM)
Protein and Healthy Fats:
Example: Cottage cheese with sliced veggies, or a handful of
almonds.
Dinner (6:30 PM)
Balanced Meal:
Lean Protein: Baked salmon, turkey, or a plant-based protein.
Complex Carbs: Whole grain pasta, brown rice, or sweet potatoes.
Vegetables: A large serving of mixed vegetables.
Healthy Fats: Olive oil, nuts, or seeds.
Evening Routine (8:00 PM)
Light Activity:
Optional: A short walk or light stretching to wind down.
Hydrate and Snack (8:30 PM):

Casein Protein: Slow-digesting protein to support muscle
recovery overnight.
Example: Cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, or a casein protein
shake.
Prepare for Bed (9:30 PM):

Relaxation: Engage in a calming activity like reading or
meditation.
Sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep to ensure proper
recovery and muscle growth.
Additional Tips
Hydration: Drink water throughout the day, aiming for at least
2-3 liters.
Consistency: Stick to your workout and meal schedule
consistently for the best results.
Progressive Overload: Gradually increase the weights and
intensity of your workouts to continually challenge your
muscles.
